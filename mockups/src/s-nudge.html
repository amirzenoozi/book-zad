Dune: Part Two — a staggering sci-fi epic

Film · 2h 46m · Tomatometer 92% · By the Reviews Desk

Denis Villeneuve returns to Arrakis with a film that is bigger, bolder and more assured than its predecessor. The cast is uniformly excellent, the score thunders, and the desert has never looked so alive — or so dangerous.

It is the rare sequel that deepens everything the first film set up: the politics, the prophecy, and the slow, terrible making of a leader. A cinema-first spectacle worth the wait.
