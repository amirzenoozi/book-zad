Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

BookZad is designed to collect no personal data. There is no account and no server.

What it accesses

  • Your bookmarks — to display, score, search and organise them.
  • The active tab's URL and title — to drive the toolbar badge and quick actions.
  • The text of pages you visit — read locally so it can be compared against your saved bookmarks for the similarity nudge.

Where data goes

Nowhere. BookZad makes no network request to any external service.

  • Page text is tokenised and compared on your device with a local TF-IDF model; the text itself is never uploaded.
  • Your notes, tags, scores and settings live in the browser's own extension storage on your device.
  • Usage signals (visit counts, recency, dwell time) are tracked by the extension itself — BookZad does not use the browser history permission.

Removing your data

Uninstalling the extension removes all stored metadata. Your native bookmarks are left untouched.
