A new way to keep your bookmarks

Built on your native bookmarks — they stay usable everywhere else.

🔎

Powerful search

Find any bookmark instantly by title, URL, notes or tags — no more digging through folders.

🗂️

Folders & quick-add

Browse with breadcrumbs, create and rename folders, move a bookmark — or a whole folder — anywhere, and save the current page into a folder in one click.

⭐

Scoring & ranking

Every bookmark gets a score from how you actually use it — visits, recency, dwell time — with a manual override. Sort by what matters.

📝

Notes & tags

Annotate and tag any bookmark. Notes and tags are searchable — and sharpen the suggestions.

🧹

Smart cleanup

Duplicates, stale bookmarks and dead links — surfaced in a tidy panel and removed in a click, keeping the copy you value most.

✨

Similarity nudge

As you browse, BookZad compares the page against your folders and nudges you to file it — or revisit what's there. Mute any site in a click; search engines are quiet by default.

Private by design

BookZad has no account and no server. The similarity check reads the page you're on only to compare it — on your device — against your bookmarks. Nothing is ever transmitted, and you can export a full backup of your bookmarks, notes, tags and scores whenever you like.
